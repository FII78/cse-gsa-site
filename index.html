About

Grad school is easier with a network behind you.

CSE-GSA makes graduate life in Computer Science and Engineering less isolating and easier to navigate. Every CSE grad student is already a member. There is nothing to pay.

A community that gets it

Peers across every lab who understand quals, advisors, and the long middle of a PhD.

Preparation for what comes next

Career panels, resume clinics, mock interviews, and alumni who took both the industry and faculty paths.

Support you can use

Wellness programming, peer mentorship, and a resource library for finding the right office the first time.

What we do

Four kinds of programming, all year.

Social

Game nights and mixers

Board games, video games, picnics, and low-key hangouts.

Professional

Panels and workshops

Resume reviews, mock interviews, and career panels with alumni in industry and academia.

Research

Lightning talks

Five-minute talks and reading groups across labs.

Wellness

Balance and belonging

Mindfulness sessions, group walks, inclusive programming, and a grad voice in department committees.
